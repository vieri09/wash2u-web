Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 6, 2026

1. Introduction

Wash2U ("we", "our", or "us") operates car wash services in Malaysia. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use our services:

  • Name — to identify you as our customer
  • Phone Number — to send receipts and service notifications via WhatsApp
  • Vehicle Plate Number — to track your service history
  • Service Records — details of washes and packages purchased
  • Visit History — dates and locations of your visits

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide car wash and detailing services
  • Send digital receipts after payment
  • Remind you of upcoming appointments
  • Notify you about car maintenance (1, 2, and 3-month intervals)
  • Re-engage you with offers if you haven't visited in 90+ days
  • Maintain your service history and package balance

4. WhatsApp Messaging

We use WhatsApp Business API (provided by Meta) to send you:

  • Payment receipts (PDF)
  • Booking reminders (2 hours before appointment)
  • Car maintenance reminders
  • Promotional messages (if inactive for 90+ days)

You can opt out anytime by replying STOP to any WhatsApp message or contacting us directly.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We only share your phone number with:

  • Meta Platforms, Inc. — solely for delivering WhatsApp messages

6. Data Security

We store your data on secure servers in our local systems. Access is restricted to authorized staff only.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for 2 years from your last visit for business and tax purposes. After this period, your data will be deleted.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your data
  • Correction — update inaccurate information
  • Deletion — request removal of your data
  • Opt-out — stop receiving WhatsApp messages
